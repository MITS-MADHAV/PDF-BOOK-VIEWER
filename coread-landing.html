What's inside

Built for serious readers

Every feature is about staying focused on the text — not wrestling the interface.

📖
Page-flip rendering

Realistic book-style page turns powered by Turn.js. PDFs feel like actual books, not flat scroll containers.

✏️
Per-page annotations

Write a note on any page. It's saved automatically and loads back the moment you return to that page.

🌙
Five reading themes

Default dark, warm sepia, black & white, eye-care mode, and a full page-invert for late-night reading.

🔍
Zoom controls

Zoom in and out at will. The reader re-renders pages at the new scale so text stays crisp at any size.

⌨️
Keyboard navigation

Use arrow keys to flip pages, and press N to toggle the annotation panel — hands stay on the keyboard.

🔒
Fully local & private

Your PDF never leaves your device. Processing happens entirely in the browser — no uploads, no accounts.
